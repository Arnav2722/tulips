What is Tulips?

Tulips is the cluster name for Sciences Department A.K.A School of Sciences. The name Tulips is inspired by the beautiful and diverse tulips flowers which symbolizes Wealth, Prestige, Elegance, Grace, Affection, and Love.



The Tagline "Blooming here in the every hue,The best among the floral crew" perfectly encapsulates the essence of this cluster. Just like tulips bloom in a myriad of colors, the Tulips cluster brings together a diverse range of events, each unique and colorful in its own way.
